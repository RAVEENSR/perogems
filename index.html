About Us

Our Story Since 2006

PERO-GE.EX is a premier exporter of precious and semi-precious Sri Lankan gemstones, based in Matara, Southern Province — a region famous for its rich moonstone mines.

Since 2006, we have been supplying and exporting all kinds of cut & polished gemstones including Blue, Yellow, Pink, and White Sapphires, Hessonite Garnet, Zircons, Spinels, Moonstones, and Topaz. Every gemstone we offer is natural and of the finest quality — never synthetic, diffused, irradiated, filled, oiled, or heated to extreme temperatures.

We take immense pride in hand-selecting each gemstone, ensuring our clients receive only stones that meet our exacting standards of quality, color, and brilliance.
